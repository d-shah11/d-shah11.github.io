IoT Based Soil Testing

This was my final year project in my undergraduate term. We chose a topic where we learnt a completely new technology of IoT(Internet of Things). First we pre filled our database with existing data to let the algorithm learn from it. We collected some soil samples and measured some soil charachteristics such as moisture, temeperature, humidity and pH with the help of respective sensors. We then stored this data on free cloud tier package and ran sum computational and cleaning process. Our goal was to predict crops from the collected soil samples.
