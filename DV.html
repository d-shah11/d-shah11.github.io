Data Visualization of Netflix Content

Before taking my Data Visualization course in Summer 2020, I thought why not experiment and learn different features of Tableau and work on it. So, I decided to visualize a small data of Netflix Movies and TV Shows. I created 4 different stories where one of it showed Top 10 movies based on regions. Another story compared the most viewed TV Shows for different age groups and so on. Finally, I combined these stories into a dashboard where the viewer can analyze all 4 stories simultaneously.
